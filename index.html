1) Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Mavis Summarize collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our Chrome extension to summarize content in your browser.

We comply with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy. We do not sell your data; we do not use or transfer data for purposes unrelated to core functionality; and we do not use or transfer data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

Stateless design (no local storage): this extension does not write any information to your browser’s local storage, sync storage, cookies, or IndexedDB. The extension does not keep a “history” or “pinned summaries” locally, and it does not persist local settings. Each session starts fresh.

2) Data We Collect

2.1 Account & Authentication (PII limited to login/register)

  • Personally identifiable information (PII) is limited to what is necessary for login and registration. We do not collect other PII for extension use.

2.2 Data processed to provide core functionality

  • Website content you select: text you highlight or the page content you explicitly request to summarize. This content is processed transiently and may be transmitted to AI model providers strictly to generate a summary.
  • Runtime diagnostics (minimal): non-identifying error codes and performance signals may be generated in-memory to keep the feature working. These signals are not stored locally by the extension.

2.3 No Local Storage

The extension does not save history, preferences, or any other data locally. We do not use chrome.storage, cookies, IndexedDB, or other local persistence mechanisms.

3) How We Use Data

  • Provide core features: generate summaries using one or more AI models.
  • Service reliability: maintain performance and security with minimal, non-identifying diagnostics.
  • Account operations: authenticate you for login/register if those features are used.

AI Model Processing

To generate summaries, selected content may be sent to third-party AI model providers (or routed via a secure proxy). We transmit only the minimum necessary text to fulfill your request and instruct providers to process it solely for summarization and not to use it for training where such controls are available.

4) Sharing & International Transfers

  • No sale of data.
  • No sharing for unrelated purposes beyond core functionality.
  • Service providers & AI vendors: data may be shared with hosting and AI model providers to operate the service under strict contractual safeguards.
  • Legal & safety: information may be disclosed if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or service integrity.
  • Transfers: where cross-border processing occurs, appropriate safeguards are applied when required.

5) Data Retention

  • Summarized content: processed transiently to generate output; not retained by the extension and not stored locally.
  • Account data: minimal PII for login/register is retained only as long as needed for authentication and legal compliance.
  • Diagnostics: if any server-side logs are generated, they are limited, non-identifying, and retained only briefly for reliability and security.

6) Security

We apply administrative, organizational, and technical measures designed to safeguard information, including transport encryption and data minimization. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we continuously improve our controls.

7) Your Choices & Controls

  • Authentication: you may choose to log in/register (if offered); PII is limited to what is necessary for that purpose.
  • Model usage: you can choose whether to use the summarization feature at all; when used, content is processed transiently.
  • Uninstall: remove the extension anytime via chrome://extensions.

Note: Because the extension is stateless, there are no local settings or local history to configure or delete.

8) Regional Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights over your personal data.

EU/EEA & UK (GDPR/UK GDPR)

  • Controller: Mavis Summarize (see contact below).
  • Legal bases: performance of a contract; legitimate interests (operate and secure the service); consent where required.
  • Rights: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent.
  • Complaints: you may contact your local supervisory authority.

California (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Rights: know, access, delete, correct, and opt-out of “sale”/“sharing” of personal information. We do not “sell” personal information.

Other Regions (e.g., Brazil LGPD, Singapore PDPA, Indonesia PDP Law)

  • We honor applicable local rights, including access, correction, deletion, and consent withdrawal where required.

To exercise rights, please see Contact. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

9) Children’s Privacy

Our extension is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Effective date” above and, where required, provide additional notice (e.g., within the extension UI or listing).
